# Market feature ledger

This ledger assigns stable IDs to every distinct capability group found in the supplied RIT/RTD corpus. A row may group mechanically repeated accessors for one coherent capability, but it does not merge independent engine semantics. Full CLR member and literal inventories remain in the external evidence workspace identified by [`inventory.json`](inventory.json).

Coverage codes: `V` verified coverage; `P` partial or adjacent coverage; `N` no current coverage; `A` adapter/client-only coverage; `U` unresolved. Columns `OB`, `NBC`, `RITC`, and `Q` mean released OrderBook-rs/Bunting adapter, current NBC Rust port, audited `ritc_mm`, and current QUARCC Rust port. Confidence is confidence that the capability exists in RIT, not confidence in its hidden formula.

## Coverage rule

No row labeled `blocked-by-insufficient-evidence` is dismissed. Its owner and required evidence are explicit, and implementation must wait rather than inventing RIT equivalence. Rows labeled `external-adapter-only`, `ui-only`, `installation-only`, or `not-market-related` have an evidence-backed authority reason; the engine must still expose the authoritative data behind adapter topics where another row assigns that data to the engine.
